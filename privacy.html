💸 ExpensioAPP Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

✅ Short version: ExpensioAPP collects zero personal data. Everything stays on your device. No account, no server, no tracking.

1. Data We Collect

ExpensioAPP collects no personal data. All information you enter — expenses, income, budgets, savings goals, loans — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (browser localStorage / IndexedDB). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

2. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. We do not operate any servers that store user data. We do not have access to your financial information at any time.

3. AI Features (Optional)

If you choose to use the AI Insights feature, you must provide your own API key from a third-party AI provider (Google Gemini, Groq, Anthropic Claude, or OpenRouter). When you click "Generate Insights":

  • An anonymised summary of your spending categories is sent to your chosen AI provider using your own API key
  • We do not see, store, or process this data
  • Your API key is stored only on your device — we never access it
  • Please review the privacy policy of your chosen AI provider

4. Analytics & Tracking

ExpensioAPP contains no analytics, no crash reporting, no user tracking, no advertising, no third-party SDKs that collect data. We use no cookies for tracking purposes.

5. Permissions

ExpensioAPP requests the following device permissions:

  • Internet access — required only to load fonts, chart libraries, and for AI Insights (optional)
  • Storage — to save your expense data locally on your device

We do not request access to contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other sensitive permissions.

6. Children's Privacy

ExpensioAPP is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
